About me

I'm a game developer from Poland, currently living in Denmark with a full stack web development background (which was my daily job until 2013). As a full stack web developer, on the backend, I worked mostly with PHP, PostgreSQL (my personal favorite DB), and MySQL. Frontend was a mix of everything (js, jQuery, HTML, CSS, etc).

Game development was always my big passion and I consistently worked in Unreal Engine. I have experience with all of the versions from 1 to 5, with my first commercial UE3 game (Rise of the Triad) being released in 2013. Since then I worked on multiple games as a lead programmer as well as porting existing games to other platforms. I have a lot of experience with the certification process and porting to all Gen8 and Gen9 consoles.
